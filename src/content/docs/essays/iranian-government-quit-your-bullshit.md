---
title: Iranian Government, Quit Your Bullshit.
description: A provacative deconstruction of a regime that has failed its citizens.
overviewLabel: "Top"
---

You say your civilization is three thousand years old. It is. But **your government is 47.** Stop borrowing credibility from Cyrus the Great. He would not recognize you. He wrote the first declaration of human rights. You shoot women in the street for showing their hair. You are not him. **You are not Persia.** You are a regime standing on Persia's neck claiming to be its voice.

You say your religion gives you divine authority. **Your religion was forced on your ancestors** by a dynasty that chose it for political strategy, not spiritual truth. The Safavids didn't hear God. They heard opportunity. They needed to be different from the Ottomans, so they picked a sect and spent two centuries beating it into the population. That's not a divine mandate. That's a branding exercise with a body count. And now you stand on the result of that forced conversion and call it eternal truth. It isn't. It's politics wearing a turban.

You say your system will outlast democracy. Maybe. But right now your people are fleeing it. Not your enemies. Your people. Your doctors. Your scientists. Your poets. Your daughters. They run to the very countries you call decadent, and when they arrive, they thrive. They don't come back. You say **your civilization endures. It does — in Los Angeles. In London. In Toronto. It endures everywhere except under your rule.**

You say you stand for justice. You have a seat in parliament for your Jewish community and you parade that like a trophy. But those Jews must publicly denounce Israel to survive. They perform loyalty under surveillance and you call it coexistence. That's not justice. **That's a hostage reading a statement.**

You say "Death to America" is just a slogan. A political expression. Don't insult us. You chant it in parliament. You print it on missiles. You teach it to children. And then you come to the negotiating table and wonder why no one believes you want peace. Words mean things. **You don't get to scream for someone's death every Friday and then act wounded when they take you seriously.**

You say you stand for Palestine. Maybe you did once. But you've turned their suffering into your fuel. You fund militias in their name and call it resistance. You keep the wound open because it justifies everything — your military spending, your repression, your entire identity. If Palestine were free tomorrow, you'd panic. Because you'd have to **explain to your own people why they're still poor, still censored, still trapped — without an enemy to blame it on.**

You say you oppose a world with a single master. Fine. So does most of the world. But opposing American hegemony doesn't require building a theocracy. It doesn't require hanging dissidents from cranes. It doesn't require morality police. **You've conflated sovereignty with tyranny** and called anyone who notices a puppet of the West.

You say freedom is not your highest value. At least that's honest. But then stop pretending your people are choosing your system. They're not choosing it. They're surviving it. There's a difference. And every time they try to tell you that — in 1999, in 2009, in 2019, in 2022 — you answer with bullets. That's not order. That's fear. And **a system maintained by fear is not a civilization. It's a cage.**

You say you answer to God. That's the deepest bullshit of all. Because it means you can never be wrong. Every failure is a test. Every critic is a heretic. Every question is blasphemy. **You've built a system that is incapable of self-correction and called it divine.** But the God you claim to serve — if that God is real, and just, and merciful — would that God look at what you've built and be proud? Would that God see the woman who can't afford medicine because you spent the money on missiles? Or the journalist rotting in your prison for writing a paragraph? Or the student who wanted to study abroad but can't because you've made your country a pariah? **Is that God's plan? Or is that yours?**

You have poets who could make the world weep. You have mathematicians who shaped the foundations of science. You have a culture of hospitality so deep that strangers are treated like family. **You have seventy million people who are — by every account from anyone who has actually met them — warm, brilliant, generous, and proud.**

And you are wasting them.

**The real crime is that you took one of the most extraordinary populations on earth and buried them under a system built on a religion their ancestors didn't choose, governed by a theory most clerics rejected, organized around the destruction of a country that exists because the world failed a people who had nowhere else to go, and sealed shut by the claim that God said so.**

*Quit your bullshit. Your people deserve better. And somewhere, underneath all the slogans and the missiles and the turbans, you know it.*
